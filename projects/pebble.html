Pebble

Tools: React Native, Expo | Source Code: GitHub

I designed & developed Pebble in collaboration with 3 other classmates during CS 147: Introduction to Human-Computer Interaction Design. Pebble is a mobile app designed for partners of pregnant individuals. It empowers them to easily acquire digestible information and recommendations so that they can better support their partner through the pregnancy journey.
