Pebble

Tools: React Native, Expo | Source Code: GitHub

Summary

I designed & developed Pebble ğŸ§in collaboration with my 3 wonderful teammates (Jessica, Divya, and Grace) during CS 147: Introduction to Human-Computer Interaction Design. Throughout our need-finding process, we heard that pregnant people often wish their partners were more connected to the pregnancy process, and likewise, partners wish they had access to information to carry out their own research. After several rounds of prototyping, we developed Pebble, a mobile app designed for partners of pregnant individuals. It empowers them to easily acquire digestible information and recommendations so that they can better support their partner through the pregnancy journey. Check out a demo video of our final app here and read on to learn more about the process of developing Pebble!
