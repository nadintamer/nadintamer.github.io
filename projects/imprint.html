Imprint

Tools: Swift

Imprint is an iOS app that incentivizes people to adopt eco-friendly behavior through missions and rewards. It was created by Team HisarCS for the Technovation Challenge. As lead programmer, I was responsible for the majority of development on Imprint.

Since this was my first time building a full iOS app, I experienced a steep learning curve. Within a month or two, I learned about different UI elements such as TableViews, how to send notifications, how to work with maps, and how to build a cohesive app.

Looking back, there are a LOT of things I would change about Imprint. The UI and UX could be vastly improved by getting rid of the colored backgrounds and simplifying the design. I also didn't know much about AutoLayout while developing Imprint, so the user experience varied heavily from one device to another. That being said, I'm still proud of the hard work I put into developing my first, full-fledged iOS app!

Imprint was selected as a Technovation 2018 Semifinalist, and I also had the opportunity to pitch the app to more than 1500 business leaders, entrepreneurs, and policy-makers at the 2019 Impact2 conference.
