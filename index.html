About Me

Hi there! 👋My name is Nadin, and I'm a sophomore at Stanford University studying Computer Science (focusing on Human-Computer Interaction) & Education.

Writing code empowers me to make positive impact on the world--I believe strongly in applying technology to social good. I'm especially passionate about the intersection of CS & education!

Last summer, I worked at Stanford HCI Group as an undergraduate researcher building StoryCoder, an iOS app that teaches young children computational thinking through storytelling activities. I'm also involved with Stanford Code the Change, where I contribute to Oppia, an open-source educational website.

Besides programming, I enjoy playing games on my Nintendo Switch, reading murder mysteries, and going to the gym.
